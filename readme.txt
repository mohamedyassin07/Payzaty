Official Payzaty WooCommerce plugin

== Description ==

Sell online and collect your money from your customers by Mada, VISA and MasterCard through your WooCommerce store with our official plugin.

== How to set Payzaty settings? ==

After installing the plugin, You can set Payzaty settings from

* Your website dashboard side bar >  *WooCommerce* > *Settings* > *Payments tab*.
* Enable *Payzaty*.
* Then go to *setup/manage* to set the settings.

== Installation ==

1. Go to `Plugins` in the Admin menu
2. Click on the button `Add new`
3. Search for `Payzaty` and click 'Install Now' or click on the `upload` link to upload `payzaty.zip`
4. Click on `Activate plugin`

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does Payzaty support a sandbox environment? =
Yes, You can enable/disable it from your Payzaty settings page.
